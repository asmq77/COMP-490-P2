06/22/16: Center of the Earth!

While visiting Old Faithful in YellowStone National Park, I saw a strange glow in the center of the geyser. Against my will, I was pulled to it by a magical force. Before I knew it, I was in the center of the earth. I started exploring and came across an open door shimmering like the the stars during a clear night. I sat down and stared for a while, unsure whether to enter. I suddenly fell asleep.

06/22/16: Center of the Moon!

I woke up from a one hour nap and found my self staring at the shimmering door that I had seen before I fell asleep. I yelled at the top of my lungs, YOLO, and ran through the door. Upon entering, I appeared in a place full of cheese, giant wheels of glowing, sparkling cheese and crators full of wine so old it stopped ageing. I knew that there was only one place in the universe where this could be possible, the center of the Moon!
